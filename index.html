General Information

The Lake Wildwood Men's 9-Hole Golf Club was founded and chartered in November 1985. It serves those who enjoy playing 9 holes of golf with other golfers in a very friendly and informal atmosphere. Membership is made up of male golfers at all levels of ability and experience. Level of play is not as important as the camaraderie of having a good time playing with old or new friends on our beautiful Lake Wildwood golf course. Our regular schedule is to play 9 holes of golf every Tuesday morning at 11:00 AM with a shotgun start, i.e., golfers play in groups of three or four, and each group starts play at 11:00 AM at an assigned tee. Usually, the third Tuesday of each month is dedicated to tournament play except in the month of December. Tournaments range in format from conventional individual and team stroke play to unconventional, e.g. playing with only 3 clubs, counting only your putts, etc. Small monetary prizes are awarded which are used for the purchase of Lake Wildwood Pro Shop merchandise. No tournament fees are assessed. Presently the annual dues are $70. Anyone interested in joining the Men's 9-Hole Golf Club, or who desires more information about the club, is welcome to contact the Membership Chairman Dan Kyte.
